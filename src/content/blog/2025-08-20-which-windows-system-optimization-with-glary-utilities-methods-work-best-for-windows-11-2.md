Why should you be careful with registry cleaning? The Windows registry is the brain of your operating system. Accidentally deleting the wrong registry entries can cause programs to stop working or prevent Windows from starting correctly. Many beginners assume that the more entries they delete, the faster their computer will run. This is a mistake.

How should beginners manage startup programs? A very common mistake is ignoring startup programs. Over time, many applications add themselves to startup, which slows down boot times significantly. Beginners often disable random items in Task Manager without understanding what they are, sometimes turning off drivers or essential background services.

Is defragmentation still necessary on Windows 11? Another common mistake is running disk defragmentation on a solid-state drive (SSD).
