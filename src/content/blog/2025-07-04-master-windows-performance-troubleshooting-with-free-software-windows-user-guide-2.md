Why Does My Windows PC Get Slow Over Time?

Several factors can cause your Windows computer to slow down:

\- Unnecessary files and temporary data clutter the hard drive. - Too many programs run at startup. - Outdated or corrupt system files. - Fragmented hard drive (on older PCs with HDDs). - Malware or unwanted software.

Addressing these issues can make your PC feel like new again.
