Why is Performance Troubleshooting Important?

Over time, a Windows computer can become sluggish due to accumulated junk files, unnecessary startup programs, and fragmented data on your hard drive. Performance troubleshooting helps identify these issues and keeps your system running smoothly by cleaning up and repairing necessary components.
