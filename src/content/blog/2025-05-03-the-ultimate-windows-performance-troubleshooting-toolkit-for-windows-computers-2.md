What Common Issues Cause Performance Slowdowns?

1\. Startup Overload: Too many programs launching at startup can significantly slow down system boot times. 2. Registry Errors: Corrupt or redundant entries in the Windows registry can impact performance. 3. Disk Fragmentation: Although less common with SSDs, fragmentation can still affect traditional HDDs. 4. Unnecessary Files and Bloatware: Accumulation of temporary files and unused software can bog down the system. 5. Malware and Viruses: Malware can steal resources and slow down your computer.
