Why is System Optimization Important for Windows PCs?

PCs slow down over time due to:

\- Accumulation of temporary files and cache - Fragmented or bloated system registries - Startup programs that run in the background - Leftover files from uninstalled programs - Outdated or corrupted system files

Without regular maintenance, these issues can cause reduced performance, system errors, and even crashes. Optimizing your system helps:

\- Improve overall speed and responsiveness - Reduce errors and crashes - Free up valuable disk space - Extend the life of your hardware
