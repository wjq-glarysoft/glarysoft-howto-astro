Why Tweak Windows Visual Effects?

Section for Advanced Users: Fine-Tuning for Maximum Benefit

How Can You Customize Windows Visual Effects in Detail?

If you want more control or wish to edit settings outside Glary Utilities, you can adjust visual effects directly in Windows, too. Here’s how:

Step 1: Access System Properties

1\. Press Windows + R, type sysdm.cpl, hit Enter. 2. Go to the “Advanced” tab. 3. Click “Settings” under the Performance section.

Step 2: Customize Visual Effects

A window opens with a list of effects such as:

\- Animate windows when minimizing and maximizing - Fade or slide menus into view - Show thumbnails instead of icons

Uncheck effects you don’t need, then click “OK.”
