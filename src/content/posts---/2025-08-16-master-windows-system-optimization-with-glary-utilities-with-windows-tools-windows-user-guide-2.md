Why is system optimization necessary? Windows PCs naturally slow down over months of use. Residual files from uninstalled software, registry fragments, duplicate files, unnecessary background programs, and fragmented hard drives all reduce performance. Optimizing your system not only improves responsiveness but also extends hardware lifespan and reduces the likelihood of errors.
