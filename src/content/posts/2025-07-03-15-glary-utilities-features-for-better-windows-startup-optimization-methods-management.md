Why Is Startup Optimization Important?

Every program set to launch at startup consumes precious system resources—RAM, CPU, and even disk performance. Over time, unnecessary or unwanted programs accumulate, cluttering your startup process and slowing down your PC. Startup optimization means reviewing, disabling, or delaying these programs so only what’s essential runs at boot.
