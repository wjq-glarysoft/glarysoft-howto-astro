What is CPU Performance Tuning and Why Does it Matter?

The CPU (Central Processing Unit) is like your computer’s brain, handling most of the calculations and operations. If it’s overloaded by background tasks, unnecessary startup programs, or system clutter, your PC slows down. CPU performance tuning means adjusting system settings and removing unnecessary processes to make sure your computer runs smoothly and efficiently.
