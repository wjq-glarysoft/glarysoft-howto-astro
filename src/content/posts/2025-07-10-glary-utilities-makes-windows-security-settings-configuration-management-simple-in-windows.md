Windows security settings help protect your computer from viruses, hackers, and unwanted access to your personal files. These settings include your firewall, automatic updates, account passwords, and privacy options like app permissions. Keeping these settings properly configured reduces the risk of malware infections, data theft, and unauthorized access.
