Why Should You Optimize and Maintain Windows 11?

Over time, your computer can slow down due to unused files, background programs, and clutter. Regular optimization keeps your system running faster, reduces crashes, and can even extend the life of your hardware. Maintenance also protects your data and ensures Windows 11 stays secure and up to date.

What Are the Easiest Ways to Clean Junk Files and Free Up Space?

Windows accumulates temporary files, cache, and old updates that eat up valuable disk space. Cleaning them out can make a noticeable difference.

Using Windows’ Built-in Tools: 1. Open Settings from the Start Menu. 2. Go to System and then Storage. 3. Turn on Storage Sense. This automatically removes temporary files and empties your Recycle Bin on a schedule. 4. Click on Temporary Files to review and delete unnecessary files manually.

How Can You Improve Startup Speed?

A slow startup is often caused by too many programs launching at boot. Disabling unnecessary startup items is easy:

1\. Press Ctrl + Shift + Esc to open Task Manager. 2. Click the Startup tab. 3. Right-click any program you don’t need to launch at startup and select Disable.

What About Keeping Your System Updated and Error-Free?

Windows Update ensures your PC stays secure and efficient, but sometimes drivers or system files get out of date.

1\. Go to Settings > Windows Update and click Check for updates. 2. Install all available updates.
