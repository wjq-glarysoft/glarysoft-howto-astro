3\. Shortcut Fixer

Why Fix Broken Shortcuts? Broken shortcuts clutter your desktop and start menu, leading to inefficiencies.

How Glary Utilities Simplifies Shortcut Repair: - Under the “Modules” tab, go to “Clean Up & Repair.” - Select “Shortcut Fixer” to scan your system for invalid shortcuts. - Review the results and remove or repair the shortcuts as necessary.

4\. File Repair

Repairing Files with Glary Utilities: - Access the “Modules” section and choose “Clean Up & Repair.” - Click on “File Repair” to pinpoint and fix corrupted files. - Follow the prompts to complete the repair process.
