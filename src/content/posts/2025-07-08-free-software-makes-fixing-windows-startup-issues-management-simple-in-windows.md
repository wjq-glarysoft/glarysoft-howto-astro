Why Do Windows Startup Issues Occur?

Startup issues can arise from several sources: - Accumulated junk files or broken shortcuts - Corrupted system files - Conflicting or unnecessary startup programs - Malware or unwanted software - Registry errors

Left unaddressed, these problems can significantly slow your computer or prevent it from booting properly.
