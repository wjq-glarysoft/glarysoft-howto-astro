What is Glary Utilities?

Its user-friendly interface makes it an excellent choice for beginners who want to maintain and optimize their systems without needing extensive technical knowledge.

2\. Installation: Run the downloaded file and follow the installation prompts. You can choose the default settings, which are suitable for most users. If prompted, allow the software to make changes to your device by clicking ‘Yes’.

How to Use Glary Utilities for System Monitoring?

1\. Overview Dashboard: Upon opening [Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com), you will see the 'Overview' tab, which provides a snapshot of your system's status. Here, you can quickly check the health of your PC, including CPU usage, memory usage, and disk space.

2\. Status Scan: Click on the ‘1-Click Maintenance’ tab for a quick health scan of your system. This feature checks for issues such as broken shortcuts, registry errors, and unnecessary startup programs. Simply click ‘Scan for Issues’ to begin.

3\. Analyze Results: After the scan, [Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com) will present you with a list of issues found. You can review these issues to understand what may be affecting your system performance.

4\. Repair Selected: Once you have reviewed the issues, click ‘Repair Problems’ to fix them. This action will clean up your system and potentially improve its speed and performance.

How to Monitor System Performance?

1\. Advanced Tools: Navigate to the ‘Advanced Tools’ section to access more in-depth system monitoring features.

2\. Process Manager: Click on 'Process Manager' to see all running processes on your computer. This can help you identify any programs that are using excessive system resources.

3\. Startup Manager: The Startup Manager allows you to view and manage programs that automatically start with Windows. Disabling unnecessary startup programs can significantly improve boot time and overall performance.

4\. Disk Usage: Use the ‘Disk Analysis’ tool to get a detailed view of how your disk space is being used. This can help you identify large files or folders that you may want to remove to free up space.
