Common Mistake: Aggressively cleaning the registry, leading to application errors or Windows instability.

Actionable Advice: Use Glary Utilities’ Registry Cleaner, but stick to the recommended or “Safe Mode” cleaning levels. Avoid manually deleting registry entries unless you’re sure of their purpose.
