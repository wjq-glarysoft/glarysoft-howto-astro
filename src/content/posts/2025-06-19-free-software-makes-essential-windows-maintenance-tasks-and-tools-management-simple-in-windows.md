Why Is Regular Windows Maintenance Important?

Over time, every Windows system accumulates digital clutter: temporary files, invalid registry entries, leftover program files, and more. This clutter can slow down your computer, cause errors, and make troubleshooting more difficult. Maintenance tasks such as cleaning junk files, defragmenting drives, updating software, and monitoring system health are essential for optimal performance.
