Why Is Disk Cleanup and Optimization Essential?

Even with robust hardware, unnecessary files, outdated system logs, and residual application data can slow your system and waste storage space. Fragmented drives (on HDDs), bloated registries, and leftover temporary files not only reduce efficiency but may also cause application errors or crashes. Disk cleanup and optimization processes address these issues, ensuring your Windows machine operates smoothly and reliably.
