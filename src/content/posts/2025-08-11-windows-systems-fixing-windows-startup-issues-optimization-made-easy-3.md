Why Do Windows Startup Issues Occur?

Startup problems often originate from the following causes: - Accumulation of unnecessary startup programs and services. - Corrupt or missing system files. - Registry errors. - Malware or unwanted software. - Faulty drivers or hardware conflicts.

Addressing these factors with a structured approach ensures a smoother and faster Windows boot experience.
