How to use it: Open Glary Utilities and navigate to the “Registry Repair” tool. Click “Scan for Issues.” After the scan, a list of problematic entries will appear. Review the list (you can expand each error for more detail), then click “Repair” to remove or fix the invalid entries.

2\. Disk Repair

Why do it? Damaged sectors or logical errors on your hard drive can lead to file corruption and system crashes.

How to use it: In Glary Utilities, find the “Disk Repair” tool under the “Advanced Tools” section. Select the drive you want to check, then click “Scan.” Follow the prompts to fix any errors found.
