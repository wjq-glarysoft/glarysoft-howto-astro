A full hard drive can make your PC sluggish. Glary Utilities helps you quickly identify and delete unnecessary files.

Step-by-step: - In Glary Utilities, go to the “Advanced Tools” tab. - Click “Disk Cleaner.” - Select the drives you want to clean (usually “C:” is the main one). - Click “OK” to start scanning. - Review the list of files found. How Do You Manage Startup Programs for Faster Boot Times?

Step-by-step: - In Glary Utilities, go to the “Advanced Tools” tab. - Click “Startup Manager.” - You’ll see a list of programs set to start with Windows. - To disable a program, uncheck its box or select it and click “Disable.” - Only keep necessary items like your antivirus.
