Keeping your Windows 11 computer running smoothly doesn’t have to be a mystery. Whether you’re new to PCs or just want a reliable maintenance routine, the right system tools and tasks can help prevent frustrating slowdowns and keep your computer in top shape. This guide introduces you to the 15 most effective, beginner-friendly maintenance strategies—plus the best tools to make each job easy.

Why Should You Maintain Windows 11 Regularly?

Regular maintenance helps your PC start faster, run smoother, and avoid common problems like crashes or unnecessary clutter. Even if you’re not tech-savvy, performing these basic tasks using built-in tools or all-in-one utilities like Glary Utilities can make a big difference.

Which Maintenance Tasks Are Most Important for Beginners?

Here are the 15 essential tasks you should include in your Windows 11 maintenance routine, along with recommended tools and easy instructions:

1\. Check for Windows Updates

Keeping Windows updated ensures you get the latest security patches and features.

How-to: Go to Settings > Windows Update > Check for updates and install any available updates.

2\. Uninstall Unused Programs

Unused programs take up space and can slow down your computer.

How-to: Go to Settings > Apps > Installed apps. Click the three dots next to programs you don’t need and select Uninstall.

Glary Utilities also offers a faster bulk uninstall option through its “Uninstall Manager.”

3\. Clean Up Disk Space

Temporary files and system junk waste valuable storage space.

How-to: Use Windows’ built-in Disk Cleanup tool: Search for “Disk Cleanup” in the Start menu, select your drive, and follow the prompts.

For a more thorough cleanup, Glary Utilities’ “Disk Cleaner” finds and removes even more junk files in just a few clicks.

4\. Defragment and Optimize Drives

Fragmented hard drives can slow down data access (note: SSDs don’t need defragmentation).

How-to: Type “Defragment and Optimize Drives” in the Start menu, select your drive, and click Optimize.

Glary Utilities includes a “Disk Defrag” tool with a simple one-button optimizer.

5\. Scan for Viruses and Malware

Protect your PC from harmful software with regular scans.

How-to: Open Windows Security > Virus & threat protection > Quick scan.

Glary Utilities’ “Malware Remover” provides an additional layer of protection, especially for finding unwanted programs.

6\. Manage Startup Programs

Too many startup programs can slow down boot time.

How-to: Press Ctrl+Shift+Esc to open Task Manager, go to the Startup tab, and disable unnecessary programs.

[Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com)' “Startup Manager” gives you an easy list to enable or disable programs at startup.

7\. Update Device Drivers

Outdated drivers can cause hardware problems or crashes.

How-to: Go to Settings > Windows Update > Advanced options > Optional updates > Driver updates.

Glary Utilities helps identify outdated drivers through its “Driver Manager.”

8\. Back Up Important Data

Always keep a backup of your important files to prevent data loss.

How-to: Use Windows’ “File History” feature (Settings > Update & Security > Backup) or use an external drive.

Glary Utilities helps schedule regular backups of your documents, photos, and other files.

9\. Monitor System Performance

Keep an eye on your system’s health and performance.

How-to: Open Task Manager (Ctrl+Shift+Esc) and check the Performance tab.

[Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com)’ “System Information” tool summarizes system health and notifies you of any issues.

10\. Remove Duplicate Files

Duplicate files waste storage and make file management harder.

How-to: Glary Utilities’ “Duplicate Finder” scans for and removes duplicate documents, photos, and music files easily.

11\. Clean and Repair the Registry

A cluttered registry can cause errors and slowdowns.

How-to: Glary Utilities’ “Registry Cleaner” safely removes obsolete entries and fixes minor registry errors.

12\. Optimize System Settings

Tweaking certain settings can improve speed and stability.

How-to: Glary Utilities’ “1-Click Maintenance” optimizes system settings, clears cache, and applies performance tweaks automatically.

13\. Secure Delete Confidential Files

Deleting files normally doesn’t remove them permanently.

How-to: Use Glary Utilities’ “File Shredder” to securely erase sensitive files, making them unrecoverable.

14\. Schedule Automatic Maintenance

Automating tasks ensures regular maintenance without reminders.

How-to: Windows 11 schedules some maintenance automatically. With Glary Utilities, you can schedule disk cleanups, registry scans, and more on a weekly or monthly basis.

15\. Restore System Health Points

Create restore points before major changes to easily undo problems.

How-to: Type “Create a restore point” in the Start menu, select your main drive, and click Create.
