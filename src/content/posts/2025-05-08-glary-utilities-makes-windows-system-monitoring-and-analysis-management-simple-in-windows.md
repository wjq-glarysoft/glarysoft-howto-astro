Why Is System Monitoring Important?

System monitoring involves keeping an eye on various aspects of your computer’s performance, such as CPU usage, memory consumption, disk activity, and network traffic. By understanding these components, you can identify potential issues before they escalate into bigger problems, ensuring your PC runs efficiently.
