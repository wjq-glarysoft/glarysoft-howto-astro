Why is Disk Cleanup Important?

Disk cleanup is essential because it removes unnecessary files that clutter your system, such as temporary files, system cache, and old backups. These files can occupy valuable space on your hard drive, slowing down your computer's performance. By regularly performing disk cleanup, you ensure that your system remains responsive and efficient.
