Tip 2: How Do I Defrag My Hard Drive for Better Performance?

For Beginners: Disk Defrag helps reorganize fragmented data so your PC can access files more quickly. To defrag your hard drive: 1. Open Glary Utilities and go to the "Advanced Tools" tab. 2.
