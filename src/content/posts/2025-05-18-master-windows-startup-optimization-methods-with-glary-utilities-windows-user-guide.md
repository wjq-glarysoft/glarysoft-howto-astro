What is Windows Startup Optimization?

Windows Startup Optimization involves managing the programs that automatically run when your computer boots up. These startup programs can significantly impact the speed and efficiency of your system. By optimizing these, you can reduce boot time and improve your computer's performance.

Why is Optimizing Startup Important?

Every program that starts with Windows consumes system resources, potentially slowing down your computer. Unnecessary programs can lead to longer startup times and reduced performance. By controlling what starts with your computer, you can ensure a faster, more efficient system.
