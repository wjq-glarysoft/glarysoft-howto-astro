Optimization for Beginners

What is '1-Click Maintenance'?

The '1-Click Maintenance' feature is perfect for beginners, offering a simple way to perform essential system cleanups quickly.

How to Use '1-Click Maintenance'?

\- Open [Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com) and navigate to the '1-Click Maintenance' tab. - By default, it will include tasks such as cleaning temporary files, repairing registry issues, and managing startup items. - Click 'Scan for Issues'. Glary Utilities will analyze your system and present a list of potential optimizations. - After reviewing the findings, click 'Repair Problems' to implement the changes.

How to Manage Startup Programs?

\- Go to the 'Startup Manager' under the 'Optimize & Improve' section. - You'll see a list of programs that start with Windows. Disable unnecessary programs to speed up boot time by unchecking their boxes.

Advanced Optimization Techniques

How to Customize System Tweaks?

Advanced users can fine-tune system settings for optimal performance.

\- Select 'Modules' from the [Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com) menu. - Under 'Optimize & Improve', choose 'System Tweaker'. - Review available tweaks such as enhancing network speed or configuring power settings. - Apply the tweaks that align with your usage requirements.

How to Use Disk Defrag?

Disk fragmentation can slow down your system. Glary Utilities includes a Disk Defrag tool for optimization.

\- Navigate to 'Modules' > 'Optimize & Improve' > 'Disk Defrag'. - Click 'Analyze' to check the fragmentation level of your drives. - If fragmentation is high, click 'Defrag' to consolidate fragmented files and improve access speed.

How to Clean Your Registry Manually?

\- Under 'Modules', select 'Clean & Repair' and then 'Registry Cleaner'. - Click 'Scan for Issues' to identify registry errors. - Review the scan results and click 'Repair' to fix the issues. It's advisable to back up your registry before making changes.
