What is Data Encryption and Why is it Important?

Data encryption converts readable data into an encoded format that can only be decoded with a specific decryption key. This process ensures that even if data is intercepted, it cannot be understood without the correct authorization. Encryption is vital for protecting personal files, financial records, and any sensitive information stored on your device from cyber threats.
