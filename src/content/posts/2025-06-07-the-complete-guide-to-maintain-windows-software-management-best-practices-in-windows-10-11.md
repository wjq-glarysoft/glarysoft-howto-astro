Why Is Software Management Important?

Beginner Section: Essential Practices for Everyday Users

How Do I Easily Install and Uninstall Programs?

Windows 10 & 11 make installing and uninstalling straightforward:

\- To install: Download software from trusted sources and follow the setup prompts. - To uninstall: Go to Settings > Apps > Apps & Features, select the program, and click Uninstall.

Tip: Avoid downloading programs from unknown sites to reduce the risk of malware.

How Can I Keep My Software Updated with Minimal Effort?

Should I Use System Restore Points?

Absolutely. Before making major changes, create a system restore point: 1. Search “Create a restore point” in the Start menu. 2. Click Create and name your restore point. This allows you to easily roll back if something goes wrong.
