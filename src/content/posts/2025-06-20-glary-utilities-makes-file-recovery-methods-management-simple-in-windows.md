When files are deleted in Windows, they aren't always lost forever. The system typically marks the space as available, while the original data remains on the disk until overwritten. This creates an opportunity for recovery—provided you act quickly and use the right tools.

How Can You Maximize Your Chances of Successful File Recovery?

Time is of the essence with file recovery—don’t install new programs or save new files to the affected drive before attempting recovery. Always restore files to a different physical drive or partition.
