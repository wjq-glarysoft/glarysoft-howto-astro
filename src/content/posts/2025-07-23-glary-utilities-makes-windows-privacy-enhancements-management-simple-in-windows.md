Why Should You Care About Windows Privacy Settings?

Windows operating systems regularly collect diagnostic data, application usage, location information, and even search history. While some of this data helps Microsoft improve user experience, it also raises legitimate privacy concerns. Unchecked, unwanted data collection can expose sensitive information and impact your overall security. By actively managing these settings, you can control what information leaves your PC and who has access to it.
