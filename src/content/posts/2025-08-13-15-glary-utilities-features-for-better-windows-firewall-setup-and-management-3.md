Why Is Windows Firewall Important?

Your firewall acts as a barrier between your computer and external threats by controlling network traffic. A poorly configured firewall can leave your system vulnerable to malware, ransomware, and hackers. That’s why combining Windows’ built-in firewall with the specialized tools found in Glary Utilities can provide comprehensive protection and help you streamline privacy and security management.
