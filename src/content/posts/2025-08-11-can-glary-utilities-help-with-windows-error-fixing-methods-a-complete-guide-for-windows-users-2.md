What Are Common Windows Errors and Why Do They Happen?

Many Windows errors are caused by leftover files, broken shortcuts, unnecessary startup programs, or a cluttered registry. Some typical examples include:

\- Slow startup or shutdown - Frequent application crashes - Error messages like “DLL file missing” or “Windows cannot find…” - Programs not opening or responding

These problems often happen because your system accumulates unnecessary files and registry entries over time. Regular cleanup and repair can prevent most of these issues.
