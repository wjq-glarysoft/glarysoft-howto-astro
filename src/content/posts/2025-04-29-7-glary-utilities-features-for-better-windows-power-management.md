What is Power Management in Windows?

Power management in Windows involves configuring settings that regulate how your computer uses energy. This includes adjusting how long it takes for your computer to go to sleep, how much power your hardware components use, and when to turn off the display. Properly configured power settings can save energy, reduce heat output, and extend the lifespan of your hardware components.
