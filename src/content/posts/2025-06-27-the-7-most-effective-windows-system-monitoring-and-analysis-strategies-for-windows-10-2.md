How Can I Monitor System Performance Using Task Manager?

Task Manager is your first line of defense for spotting slowdowns.

Step 1: Right-click the taskbar and select Task Manager. Step 2: Go to the Performance tab to see real-time CPU, memory, disk, and network usage. Step 3: Click the Processes tab to identify apps or processes using the most resources.

Example: If your PC feels sluggish, check if any program is consuming excessive CPU or memory. You can right-click and "End task" for unnecessary apps.

What About Monitoring Startup Programs?

Too many startup programs can slow boot times and waste resources.

Step 1: Open Task Manager and go to the Startup tab. Step 2: Review the list of programs and their impact ratings. Step 3: Right-click unnecessary programs and choose Disable.
