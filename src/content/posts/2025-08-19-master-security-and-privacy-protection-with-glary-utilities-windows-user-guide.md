Why is privacy protection essential on Windows? Windows systems store vast amounts of personal data: browsing habits, saved passwords, cookies, temporary files, and even fragments of deleted documents. These traces can be exploited by malicious software or unauthorized users. A strong privacy and security strategy eliminates data leaks, keeps files secure, and ensures sensitive activity remains confidential.
