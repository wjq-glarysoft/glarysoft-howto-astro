Why Do Windows Startup Issues Occur?

Startup problems in Windows can stem from many sources, including:

\- Too many programs launching at boot - Corrupted startup entries in the registry - Malware or unwanted software - Fragmented or bloated system files - Leftover files from uninstalled apps

Tackling these issues requires awareness, good habits, and effective tools. Here’s how you can use Glary Utilities to keep your Windows startup running smoothly.
