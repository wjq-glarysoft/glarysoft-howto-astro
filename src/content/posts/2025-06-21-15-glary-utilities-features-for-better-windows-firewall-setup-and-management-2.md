Why Is the Windows Firewall Important?

The Windows Firewall acts as a barrier between your computer and potential threats from the internet or other networks. It blocks suspicious traffic and helps prevent hackers from gaining access to your files and personal information. Keeping your firewall active and properly configured is a key step in protecting your digital life.
