Why Is System Optimization Important?

Before diving into the mistakes, let’s clarify why system optimization matters. Over time, Windows accumulates unnecessary files, outdated registry entries, and background programs that can sap performance. Regular maintenance ensures your system remains fast, secure, and reliable.
