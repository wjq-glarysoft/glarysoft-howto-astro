How Do I Perform a 1-Click Maintenance?

The 1-Click Maintenance feature is the easiest way to quickly clean and optimize your computer.

1\. Open [Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com). 2. On the main screen, make sure the “1-Click Maintenance” tab is selected. 3. By default, several items (such as Registry Cleaner, Shortcuts Fixer, Spyware Remover, Disk Repair, Tracks Eraser, and Temporary Files Cleaner) will be checked for scanning. Wait for the process to complete and restart your computer if prompted.

What Other Optimization Tools Should I Use?

While 1-Click Maintenance covers the basics, Glary Utilities offers more targeted tools for deeper optimization.

Managing Startup Programs

Too many programs starting with Windows can slow boot time. Here’s how to manage them:

1\. Click the “Startup Manager” under the “Advanced Tools” tab. 2. Review the list of programs set to start with Windows. 3. To disable a program, uncheck the box next to its name or select it and click “Disable.” 4. Only disable programs you recognize and know you don’t need at startup. For example, you might disable “iTunes Helper” or “Adobe Updater,” but leave antivirus software enabled.

Using Disk Cleanup

Glary Utilities can help you free up space by removing junk files.

1\. Go to “Advanced Tools” and select “Disk Cleaner.” 2. Click “Search” to let the tool analyze your drives for unnecessary files. 3. After the scan, review the list and click “Clean” to remove selected files.

Cleaning the Registry

An overloaded Windows registry can cause slowdowns. Here’s how to safely clean it:

1\. Under “Advanced Tools,” click “Registry Cleaner.” 2. Click “Scan Registry Issues.” 3. Once the scan completes, click “Repair Registry Issues.” 4. Glary Utilities automatically creates a backup before making changes, so you’re safe if you need to undo any edits.

Is Privacy Protection Important?

Yes, cleaning traces of your activity helps protect your privacy. Use “Tracks Eraser” found under the “Advanced Tools” tab. Check the items you wish to remove (like browser history or temporary files), then click “OK.”

How Often Should I Run Glary Utilities?

For everyday users, running the 1-Click Maintenance once a week keeps your system clean and responsive. You may use specific tools, like Disk Cleaner or Startup Manager, as needed—especially if you notice your PC slowing down.

What Should I Do If I Have Problems?

Glary Utilities includes restore and backup options for all major tools. If you accidentally disable a needed startup program or remove something important, you can usually restore it through the tool’s interface or by using Windows System Restore.
