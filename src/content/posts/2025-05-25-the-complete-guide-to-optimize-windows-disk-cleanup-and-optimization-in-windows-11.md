Why Should You Clean Up and Optimize Your Disk?

Over time, your computer accumulates temporary files, leftover data from uninstalled apps, and cached items that take up valuable disk space. These unnecessary files not only hog storage but can also slow down your PC. Regular cleaning and optimization can:

\- Free up storage space - Improve system performance - Reduce boot-up and program load times - Prevent errors and system crashes

What are the Built-In Windows 11 Tools for Disk Cleanup?

Windows 11 includes some handy utilities to help you clean up your hard drive:

1\. Storage Sense Go to Settings > System > Storage and turn on Storage Sense. This tool automatically deletes temporary files, empties the recycle bin, and clears downloads at intervals you choose.

2\. Disk Cleanup Type "Disk Cleanup" in the Start menu and select the drive you want to clean. Disk Cleanup scans for files you can safely remove, like temporary files, system logs, and Windows update leftovers.

These tools are easy to use but may not catch everything. For a deeper clean and better optimization, third-party utilities can help.

What Other Tips Can Help Keep Your PC Clean and Fast?

\- Uninstall programs you no longer use from Settings > Apps > Installed apps. - Regularly empty your Recycle Bin. - Clear your browser cache and history. - Store large files you rarely use (like old videos) on an external drive or cloud storage.
