Why Does Windows Slow Down?

Before diving into solutions, it’s important to understand the typical causes of reduced performance:

\- Accumulated junk files and system cache - Fragmented or full hard drives - Redundant or corrupt registry entries - Unnecessary startup programs - Malware or unwanted software - Outdated drivers or missing updates

Monthly clean-ups are sufficient for most users, though heavy users may benefit from weekly scans.
