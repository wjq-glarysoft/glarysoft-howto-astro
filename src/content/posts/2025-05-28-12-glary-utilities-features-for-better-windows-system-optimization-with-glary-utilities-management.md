For Beginners: Quick and Essential Optimization Tips

1\. One-Click Maintenance

Glary Utilities’ hallmark feature, "1-Click Maintenance," allows you to scan and fix several common issues with just one press. It checks for disk errors, registry problems, spyware, broken shortcuts, and temporary files in seconds.

How to use: - Open [Glary Utilities](https://www.glarysoft.com). - Click on "1-Click Maintenance." - Hit "Scan for Issues," then "Repair Problems." This single action can often free up gigabytes of space and resolve minor slowdowns without any technical know-how.

2\. Disk Cleaner

Over time, your system accumulates junk files from uninstalled programs, browser caches, and more. The Disk Cleaner feature finds and deletes these files.

How to use: - Select "Advanced Tools" > "Clean & Repair" > "Disk Cleaner." - Click "Scan," review the results, and click "Clean." Result: Faster access to files and less wasted disk space.

3\. Startup Manager

Slow boot times are often due to too many programs launching at startup. Glary Utilities’ Startup Manager makes it easy to review and disable unnecessary items.

How to use: - Go to "Optimize & Improve" > "Startup Manager." - Disable programs you don’t immediately need when Windows starts. This can shave minutes off your boot time without manual registry edits.

4\. Shortcut Fixer

Broken shortcuts clutter your desktop and start menu. Glary Utilities finds and removes them automatically.

How to use: - Click "Advanced Tools" > "Clean & Repair" > "Shortcut Fixer." - Run the scan and let Glary Utilities remove any dead links.

For Advanced Users: Deeper Optimization and Customization

5\. Registry Cleaner and Defrag

A bloated or fragmented registry can slow down your PC. Use Glary Utilities’ Registry Cleaner to remove invalid entries, and Registry Defrag to compact and optimize the registry’s structure.

Steps: - Run "Registry Cleaner" to scan and safely remove obsolete entries. - Use "Registry Defrag" to streamline the registry for faster access. Tip: Always back up your registry before making changes.

6\. Disk Defragmenter

Fragmented files slow down disk access. Glary Utilities includes a Disk Defragmenter to reorganize file data for quicker performance.

How to use: - Go to "Advanced Tools" > "Optimize & Improve" > "Disk Defrag." - Select your drive and run the defrag process. Recommended for traditional hard disks, not SSDs.

7\. Process Manager

To see which programs are hogging resources, try the Process Manager. It gives detailed information and lets you end problematic tasks on the spot.

How to use: - Open "Advanced Tools" > "System Tools" > "Process Manager." - Identify and terminate resource-heavy or suspicious processes. Helpful for troubleshooting slowdowns caused by runaway programs.

8\. Uninstall Manager

Uninstalling software through Windows Control Panel often leaves leftovers. Glary Utilities’ Uninstall Manager thoroughly removes programs, including hidden files and registry entries.

How to use: - Head to "Advanced Tools" > "Clean & Repair" > "Uninstall Manager." - Choose apps to uninstall and follow prompts for a complete cleanup.

9\. Duplicate File Finder

Duplicate files waste space and cause confusion. The Duplicate File Finder scans folders or drives for redundant copies.

How to use: - Select "Advanced Tools" > "Clean & Repair" > "Duplicate Files Finder." - Scan your selected location, review results, and safely remove duplicates. Great for cleaning up downloads or backup folders.

10\. Context Menu Manager

Too many right-click options can slow productivity. Glary Utilities allows you to control what appears in your Windows context menu.

How to use: - Go to "Advanced Tools" > "Optimize & Improve" > "Context Menu Manager." - Disable or remove unnecessary menu items for a cleaner experience.
