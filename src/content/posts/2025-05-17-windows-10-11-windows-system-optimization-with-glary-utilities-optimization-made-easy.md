What is System Optimization and Why is it Important?

System optimization involves fine-tuning your computer to perform at its best. This means freeing up resources, removing unnecessary files, and ensuring the system runs smoothly. Regular optimization can lead to faster startup times, improved application performance, and extended hardware life. For beginners, this may seem daunting, but tools like Glary Utilities simplify the process by automating many tasks.
