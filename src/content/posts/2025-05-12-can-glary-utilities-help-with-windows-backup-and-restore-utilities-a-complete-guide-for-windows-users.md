What Are Backup and Restore Utilities?

Backup and restore utilities help safeguard your data and system configuration by creating copies of your files and settings. These utilities allow you to revert your system to a previous state in case of corruption or data loss. Windows provides built-in options such as 'Backup and Restore (Windows 7)' and 'File History,' but third-party tools can offer additional features and ease of use.
